## Brand Voice Guidelines

- Technical but accessible — lead with control and privacy, not just "AI"
- Empowering tone (user control, self-sovereignty)
- Show real workflows, not abstract capabilities
- Community-oriented (open source ethos)

## Target Audiences

- **Primary**: Developer-Operators (25-45, technically proficient, privacy-conscious)
- **Secondary**: Small startup/marketing/research teams needing shared AI agents
- **Tertiary**: Open source contributors and ClawHub skill creators
