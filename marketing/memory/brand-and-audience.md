# OpenClaw Marketing — Brand & Audience Knowledge

## Brand Positioning

**Product**: OpenClaw — open-source AI agent gateway for personal and team use.
**Tagline**: "Your AI agents, your rules."
**Core Value Proposition**: Self-hosted, privacy-first AI agent orchestration that connects multiple LLM providers with messaging channels (Telegram, Slack, Discord, etc.).

### Key Differentiators

- **Open Source**: Full transparency, community-driven development
- **Multi-Provider**: OpenAI, Google, Anthropic, OpenRouter — seamless failover
- **Self-Hosted**: Data stays on your machine, no cloud dependency
- **Multi-Channel**: One agent, multiple messaging surfaces
- **Extensible**: Plugin system, ClawHub skill marketplace, custom skills

### Brand Voice

- Technical but accessible
- Empowering (user control, self-sovereignty)
- Community-oriented (open source ethos)
- Practical (real use cases, not hype)

## Target Audience

### Primary: Developer-Operators (个人开发者运营者)

- Solo developers running AI agents for personal productivity
- Technical founders automating business workflows
- Power users who want control over their AI stack
- Demographics: 25-45, technically proficient, privacy-conscious

### Secondary: Small Teams

- Startup teams needing shared AI agents
- Marketing teams automating content workflows
- Research teams aggregating multi-source analysis

### Tertiary: Open Source Contributors

- Developers contributing plugins/extensions
- Skill creators on ClawHub marketplace

## Competitor Landscape

| Competitor          | Strengths           | Weaknesses vs OpenClaw                       |
| ------------------- | ------------------- | -------------------------------------------- |
| LangChain/LangGraph | Mature ecosystem    | Framework, not runtime; no built-in channels |
| AutoGPT/AgentGPT    | Brand recognition   | Cloud-dependent, limited provider choice     |
| Botpress            | Visual builder      | Closed source, SaaS pricing                  |
| n8n + AI nodes      | Workflow automation | Not agent-native, complex setup              |
| Custom bots         | Full control        | No failover, no skill ecosystem              |

## Messaging Guidelines

- Lead with **control and privacy**, not just "AI"
- Show real workflows, not abstract capabilities
- Emphasize **cost savings** from multi-provider failover
- Community stories > feature lists
